Hemming Classic Car

  • Redesigning Hemmings Classic Car Magazine was an exercise in balancing tradition with modernity. The challenge was to respect the magazine’s nostalgic essence while enhancing its visual appeal for a contemporary audience. By focusing on typography, layout, and hierarchy, I was able to create a design that honored the magazine’s history while improving readability and engagement. This project solidified my belief that great design should preserve a brand’s authenticity while adapting to new audiences and contexts.
