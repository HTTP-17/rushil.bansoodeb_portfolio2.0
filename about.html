Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Rushil Bansoodeb, a creative graphic designer. I grew up on a tropical island called Mauritius. After completing high school, I moved to Canada to continue my studies in art and media at Humber Polytechnic, where I pursued an Advanced Diploma in Graphic Design. I enjoy exploring new challenges, whether it’s designing or facing the reality. My goal is to apply my talents to create meaningful, innovative designs while gaining valuable industry experience. What makes me unique is that I don’t consider my job just a profession but a passion. Another thing about me is that I always find ways to enjoy life for example, snowboarding in winter or skateboarding with friends
